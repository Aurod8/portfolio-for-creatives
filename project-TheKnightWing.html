The Knight Wing Bar

The Knight wing is a nightbar that serves both food and drinks. It is website made on wordpress. I created this website to learn how to use wordpress and to give a good nightly bar feel to this website.

This was a solo project that I made to practice my WordPress skills which took me around 3 weeks to create.

This is a a website of a madeup night bar. This page shows you the website and the design process behind it.

The Knight Wing Bar

These two images show the homepage and the menu page of the website.

The homepage features a bold, dark design with a striking hero image that captures the bar’s vibrant nightlife atmosphere. The menu page is clean and easy to navigate, with both pages shown in its final design.

The whole website follows the same pattern and color scheme. You will see as you scroll down the page.

The process

Now let’s take a look at the design process behind this website, and how the website evolved from the initial concept to the final design.

The design process began with brainstorming ideas and creating mood boards to establish the overall look and feel of the website. I focused on capturing the essence of a vibrant nightlife atmosphere while ensuring the design was user-friendly and accessible.
