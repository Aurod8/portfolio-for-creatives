ParkEase

ParkEase is an interactive app designed to enhance the user experience when finding a parking spot.

This was a group project case study designed to come up with an app based on a problem. Our group decied to design an app that would make it easier for users to find parking spots in busy urban areas, reducing stress and saving time.

In ths page you will see the process in how we came up with the app idea and how we developed it. The app includes features such as real-time parking availability, navigation to the nearest parking spot, and user reviews of parking locations. By providing a seamless and efficient parking experience, ParkEase aims to improve urban mobility and reduce traffic congestion.
