Shipyards NightMarket

This is a website redesign project for Shipyards NightMarket, a vibrant night market located in the heart of North Vancouver. The goal of this project was to create a visually appealing and user-friendly website that captures the essence of the night market while providing essential information to visitors.

This was a solo project completed over the course of 4 weeks. The redesign focused on improving the user experience by creating a more intuitive navigation system, enhancing the visual appeal with vibrant colors and engaging imagery, and ensuring the website is responsive across all devices. The new design successfully captures the lively atmosphere of Shipyards NightMarket while providing visitors with easy access to event details, vendor information, and other essential resources.
