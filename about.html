    Designer

    Hello Users my name is Aurod Ashrafi and I am a junior UI designer at IDEA School of Design. I am passionate about creating intuitive and engaging user experiences that solve real-world problems. I bring a unique perspective to my work, combining aesthetics with functionality. I am always eager to learn new skills and stay updated with the latest design trends to ensure that my work is innovative and impactful.

    Beyond school and Ux design, i enjoy playing soccer, going to the gym, and hanging out with friends. I am also a big fan of music and love attending concerts and festivals whenever I can. In my free time, I like to explore new places and try out different cuisines. I believe that having a well-rounded life outside of work helps me bring fresh perspectives and creativity to my design projects.

    Currently I am part of the interaction design program at CapU with a dream of using these skills to get into Architectural design. I am excited to continue growing as a designer and to see where my career takes me in the future.

    My future goals and dreams include to keep playing soccer, getting into architecture school/work, and live life to the fullest. I am only 19 years old therefore I have a lot of skills to learn for my future career and to see how I can translate them to getting into architecture school and eventually working in the field. I am excited to see where my career takes me and to continue learning and growing as a designer.
